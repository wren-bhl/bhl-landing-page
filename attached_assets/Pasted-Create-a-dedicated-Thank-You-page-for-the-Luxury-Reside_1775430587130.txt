Create a dedicated Thank You page for the “Luxury Residential / HOA” Recovery Pod landing page.

Goal:
This page appears immediately after a user submits the form on the Luxury Residential / HOA landing page. The purpose of the Thank You page is to confirm the submission, reinforce the value proposition, and get the lead to book a call through an embedded HubSpot calendar.

Brand:
BH Labs / Recovery Pod

Audience:
Luxury residential developers, HOA board members, condo association decision-makers, property managers, multifamily operators, and amenity-focused real estate executives evaluating premium wellness offerings for residents.

Tone:
Premium, polished, modern, high-converting, residential luxury-focused, wellness-driven, clean upscale aesthetic.

Key message from original landing page:
- Turn underutilized amenity or common space into a premium wellness offering
- Increase property value and resident appeal with luxury recovery experiences
- Recovery Pod helps residential communities create a differentiated amenity
- Turnkey implementation with minimal operational burden
- Elevate the resident experience with a modern wellness destination

Build the page with these sections:

1. Hero Section
- Large headline: “Thank You — Your Revenue Projection Request Has Been Received”
- Subheadline: “You’re one step closer to transforming unused amenity space into a premium wellness experience for residents.”
- Short confirmation text that BH Labs received their information and the next best step is to schedule a strategy call.

2. Booking Section
- Add a strong section headline like: “Book Your Strategy Call”
- Add short copy explaining that on the call, BH Labs will review their property, resident amenity goals, space requirements, implementation options, and rollout strategy.
- Insert a clear placeholder for the HubSpot meeting embed:

Use 3 to 4 visually clean cards with these benefit themes:
- Upgrade underused amenity space
- Premium resident wellness experience
- Increased property differentiation and appeal
- Turnkey implementation with training and support

4. What Happens On The Call
Include a short bullet section:
- Review your residential property layout
- Evaluate amenity and wellness opportunity
- Discuss equipment configuration
- Outline implementation timeline and next steps

5. Trust / Credibility Section
Include a short paragraph referencing BH Labs’ experience:
- 5 successful wellness locations across Miami

- Headline: “Secure Your Booking Time”
- Short text encouraging them to choose a time now while interest is high
- Secondary button anchor link that scrolls back to the embedded calendar:
  Button text: “Book My Call”

Design and UX requirements:
- Mobile responsive
- Visually consistent with a premium residential wellness brand
- Strong spacing, clear hierarchy, modern typography
- Use subtle wellness/luxury styling with an upscale residential feel
- Keep it conversion-focused, not cluttered

Technical requirements:
- Build as a standalone thank-you page in Replit
- Include smooth scrolling for the “Book My Call” button
- Calendar section should be prominent above the fold or very near it
- Add a fallback text link area under the embed placeholder:
  “Having trouble viewing the calendar? Contact us at info@thebiohacklab.com”

Also include:
- SEO title: “Thank You | Luxury Residential / HOA Recovery Pod | BH Labs”
- Meta description: “Thank you for requesting your Recovery Pod revenue projection. Book your strategy call with BH Labs.”
- Clean semantic HTML structure
- Modern CSS styling
- If using React, keep component structure clean and production-ready

Do not create a generic thank you page. Make it specifically aligned to luxury residential communities, HOA decision-makers, condo associations, and multifamily operators evaluating premium wellness amenities.
